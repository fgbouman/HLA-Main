VIRTUAL OFFICE @ HONG KONG >>>

 

Our Virtual Office is a complete office solution. Depending on your needs, our packages provide a Mail Drop at a street mailing address with worldwide mail forwarding, dedicated local telephone number with international call forwarding, voice mail or live answering options and local fax number without the need to be on location.

 

Why us?

  • Zero setup fees
  • Fuss-free sign up
  • Activated in 2 business days
  • 7 Days Money Back Guarantee
  • Free Storage of mails and small packages for up to 60 days
  • Payment methods: by Visa, Mastercard, American Express via Paypal, Wire Transfer, Internet Banking, Western Union, Singapore cheque or cash

 

 

Why Use A Virtual Office Solution?

 

  • You need to create a professional business image
  • You want to test a new market at manageable cost
  • You need a Registered Address to receive government and bank letters
  • You need an address, phone & fax for printing on your name cards, letterheads and any other official documents
  • You need a Singapore presence while you reside in another country
  • You are busy getting new business
  • You miss calls because you're in meetings
  • You need localized assistance
  • You work from home
