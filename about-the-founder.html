ABOUT THE FOUNDER

 

Hilda Loe has had extensive professional, managerial and executive experience in the travel, IT and business service sectors over the past twenty-eight years, working with a number of global and International companies such as Galileo International, United Airlines, local and international banks.  Her extensive exposure to global business encouraged her to add to her Masters degree in Tourism and Hospitality Management by becoming a Certified Paralegal, a practicing Chartered Secretary, an Associate Financial Planner and an Associate Estate Planning Practitioner. All of these in order to help strengthen her rapidly growing international business service company, which she founded in 2002.  These qualifications are augmented by her professional memberships in the Institute of Paralegals in UK, New York City Paralegal Association in USA, The Institute of Chartered Secretaries and Administrators in Singapore and UK, Financial Planning Association in Singapore and Estate Planning Practitioners Ltd, Singapore.

 

In conjunction with the expansion of her business into the registration of vessels, she was appointed as the Deputy Registrar for the Cook Islands, representing them in Singapore and neighboring countries.
